Executive Summary

The SFAST project was initiated to address the critical inefficiencies in processing school fee applications. By migrating from a manual, paper-based workflow to a sophisticated digital ecosystem, we resolved transparency issues and administrative bottlenecks that previously delayed funding for thousands of students. The core of the solution is a centralized hub that automates validation and approval chains, ensuring real-time accountability.

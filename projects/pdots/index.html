Executive Summary

The Document Online Tracking System (PDOTS) is a centralized digital platform designed to manage and monitor internal document workflows across departments. It digitizes the routing, approval, and tracking of documents, replacing traditional paper-based processes with a structured and traceable system. By providing real-time visibility of document status and maintaining a centralized record of transactions, the system improves processing efficiency, reduces delays, and minimizes the risk of document misplacement.

Business Challenge

Manual document routing processes created operational delays, limited tracking visibility, and increased the risk of document misplacement across departments due to reliance on physical transfer workflows.
